The Batting Cages
Entrepreneurs. Process. Values.

The Batting Cages are small, agile innovation labs focused on finding product market fit for specific market opportunities. The Batting Cage team is centered around passionate, driven product and tech capable entrepreneurs. Each Batting Cage will focus on 2-3 market problems/opportunities. The entrepreneurs will work together on finding product market fit but each market will have an entreprenur leading the approach to create and capture value. The Batting Cages will actively develop, deploy, test and interate to find product market fit.When assumptions have been validated in a given market, Missions Reds will set up a "NewCo" with the IP flowing from the Batting Cage into the "NewCo" and the entrepreneur lead in the market joining "NewCo". Mission Reds plans to create a new Batting Cage roughly every six months. While there is active interest from investors in funding the Batting Cages, financial participation in the Batting Cages is highly selective. Funding opportunities are more open as "NewCos" are spun out of the Batting Cages.
